Metadata for Source data file

Card Region: Represents the geographical region associated with the card. Used to categorize markets and tailor regional strategies.
  
Card Market: Indicates the specific market or country where the card is used. Helps in regional market analysis and targeting.

MKCID: Unique card identifier within the market. Essential for tracking and managing transactions.

Client Organisation: Name of the organization associated with the card. Useful for B2B analysis and client management.

Business Process Date: Date when the transaction or process occurred. Critical for time-based analysis and reporting.

Billed Currency: Currency in which the transaction is billed. Important for financial analysis and currency conversion.

Net Billed Amount: The amount billed in the transaction, reflecting the actual net value. Key for financial reporting and revenue tracking.
